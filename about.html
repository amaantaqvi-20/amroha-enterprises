About Amroha Enterprises

A local business built on trust, quality products, and genuine care for our customers and dealer partners.

Who We Are

Amroha's Trusted Electronics Partner

We started with a clear vision in 2020 and officially opened our store in 2022 — to bring quality electronics, genuine RO products, and reliable electrical items to the people of Amroha and surrounding areas.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name for both retail customers and wholesale dealers in the region — supplying RO water purifiers, LED TVs, washing machines, inverter batteries, and electrical goods.

Our wholesale operation focuses on RO purifiers and spare parts — serving local dealers and distributors across Amroha and nearby towns with competitive pricing and reliable supply.

How We Got Here

2020
The Vision Begins
Started planning a business that would bring quality electronics and reliable RO solutions to Amroha — a market that lacked a trusted local supplier.
2022
Official Launch
Opened Amroha Enterprises at Danishmandan, Near Bada Imambada, Amroha. Started with RO purifiers, spare parts and basic electrical items.
2023
Expanding the Range
Added LED TVs, washing machines, inverter batteries and a wider range of electrical appliances to serve more customer needs under one roof.
2024
Growing the Wholesale Network
Established ourselves as a reliable wholesale supplier of RO products and spare parts for local dealers and shops across the Amroha region.
2025–26
Going Digital
Launched our website and online presence to make it easier for customers and wholesale partners to reach us — and expanding our service coverage.
